WordPress Website Setup

Are you looking to establish your online presence with a professional website? Look no further! With my WordPress Website Setup service, I will help you kickstart your online journey by creating a fully functional WordPress website tailored to your needs.

What's Included:
  1. WordPress Installation: I will install the latest version of WordPress on your hosting server, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free setup process.
  2. Theme Installation and Configuration: Based on your preferences and requirements, I will install and configure a suitable WordPress theme that aligns with your brand identity and vision.
  3. Basic Page Setup: I will set up essential pages such as Home, About, Services, Contact, etc., to provide visitors with key information about your business or project.
  4. Plugin Installation: I will install essential plugins to enhance the functionality and features of your website, such as SEO optimization, security, and performance optimization.
  5. Customization Options: While this gig focuses on the initial setup, I can also provide basic customization options such as logo integration, color scheme adjustments, and font selection to personalize your website.
Process:
  1. Discussion: We'll start with a discussion to understand your requirements, goals, and preferences for the website.
  2. Setup: Once we have clarity on your needs, I will proceed with the setup process, including WordPress installation, theme selection, and basic configuration.
  3. Review and Revisions: After the initial setup, you'll have the opportunity to review the website and request any minor revisions or adjustments to ensure it meets your expectations.
  4. Handover: Upon completion and your satisfaction, I will provide you with the necessary credentials and documentation to manage and maintain your new WordPress website.
Why Choose Me:
  1. Expertise: With years of experience in WordPress development, I have the expertise to deliver a professional and functional website tailored to your needs.
  2. Personalized Service: I prioritize clear communication and collaboration to ensure that your vision is realized in the final product.
  3. Support: Even after the setup is complete, I'm here to provide ongoing support and assistance to help you navigate your WordPress journey with confidence.

Price: Starting from $50 (Price may vary based on additional customization or advanced requirements)
