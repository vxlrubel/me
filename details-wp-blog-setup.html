WordPress Blog Setup

Are you looking to start a blog but feeling overwhelmed by the technical aspects of setting it up? Let me take the hassle out of the process for you. With this gig, I will set up a fully functional WordPress blog tailored to your needs and preferences.

What's Included:
  1. WordPress Installation: I will install the latest version of WordPress on your hosting server, ensuring a smooth and secure setup.
  2. Theme Installation: Choose a theme from the WordPress repository or provide your own, and I will install and configure it to match your branding and style preferences.
  3. Basic Plugin Setup: Essential plugins such as Yoast SEO, Akismet (for spam protection), and WP Super Cache (for performance optimization) will be installed and configured to enhance the functionality and security of your blog.
  4. Basic Pages Creation: I will create essential pages such as Home, About, Contact, and Privacy Policy pages, helping you establish a professional online presence.
  5. Customization: Basic customization of the theme, including header and footer setup, color scheme adjustments, and font selection, to ensure your blog reflects your unique identity.

Deliverables: Upon completion, you will receive a fully functional WordPress blog ready for you to start publishing your content. You'll also receive documentation on how to manage your blog, including adding new posts, pages, and media.

Extras (Optional):
  • Premium Theme Installation: If you prefer a premium theme, I can help you install and configure it for an additional fee starting from $50.00, depending on the complexity of the theme.
  • Advanced SEO Setup: Optimize your blog for search engines with advanced SEO setup, including keyword research, meta tags optimization, and XML sitemap submission. Additional $50.00.
  • E-commerce Integration: If you plan to sell products or services through your blog, I can integrate an e-commerce solution such as WooCommerce. Additional $100.00

Why Choose Me?
With years of experience in WordPress development, I ensure not only a smooth setup process but also a blog that meets your specific requirements and goals. Let's bring your blogging aspirations to life together!
